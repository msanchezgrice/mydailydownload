# Product Strategy Deep Dive — my AI Skill Tutor (.com)

## Situation
User has a live product at myaiskilltutor.com that offers:
- Career-based AI skill modules / learning paths
- 24/7 AI tutor for career questions
- Daily AI news tailored to your field
- Suggested posts / build-log updates for public career proof
- $49.99/mo pricing with 7-day free trial
- Employer-side talent marketplace with verified proof pages
- Strong messaging around "outlearn your peers" in an era of job disruption

The user needs:
1. Competitive landscape analysis across multiple product directions
2. Product option evaluation (newsletter, chatbot, skills verification, etc.)
3. A decision matrix for MVP paths
4. Potential rebrand considerations
5. Strategic recommendations

## Skill Stack
- `deep-research-swarm`: Multi-dimensional competitive landscape research
- `report-writing`: Synthesize into a strategic analysis document

## Stages

### Stage 1 — Deep Research Swarm (parallel, multi-dimension)
Load `deep-research-swarm`. Route B — Focused Search with multiple parallel deep dives:

**Dimension A: AI Upskilling / Skill-Building Platforms**
- Platforms like Coursera, Udacity, LinkedIn Learning, Pluralsight, Codecademy, etc.
- AI-specific upskilling: DeepLearning.AI, AI TUTOR platforms, Maven, Section, etc.
- New AI-native learning: khanmigo, Duolingo Max, personalized AI tutoring

**Dimension B: AI News Aggregators & Personalized Newsletters**
- Tools like tl;dr AI, Ben's Bites, The Rundown, Superhuman, etc.
- Per-career / persona newsletter products
- AI tool aggregators (Futurepedia, TheresAnAIForThat, etc.)

**Dimension C: AI Career / Job Market Tools**
- AI resume builders, interview prep, job matching
- LinkedIn AI features, Indeed, Glassdoor AI tools
- Portfolio builders, proof-of-skill platforms (e.g., Workera, TestGorilla, HackerRank)

**Dimension D: Chatbot / AI Tutor Products**
- General AI tutors (Khanmigo, Synthesis, etc.)
- Professional/enterprise AI coaching
- Skill verification and credentialing platforms

**Dimension E: Pricing, GTM & Business Models in This Space**
- Subscription pricing benchmarks
- Freemium vs. paid conversion strategies
- B2C vs. B2B models

### Stage 2 — Strategic Synthesis & Decision Matrix
Load `report-writing`. Produce a strategic analysis covering:
1. Competitive landscape summary (all dimensions)
2. Product option evaluation (newsletter, chatbot, skills platform, hybrid)
3. Decision matrix with scoring criteria
4. MVP recommendation
5. Brand name considerations
6. Go-to-market suggestions

### Stage 3 — Deliverable
Final report as .docx delivered to user.
