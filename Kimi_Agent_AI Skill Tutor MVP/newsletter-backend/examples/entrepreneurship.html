Quick Hits

Female-founded Atheni raises £350K to assist businesses in utilizing AI

Atheni, a startup founded by women and supported by angel investors, has raised £350K to help organizations enhance their AI capabilities and business impact. The company aims to address the challenge of effectively using AI, which has become a significant concern for modern businesses.

Source: techfundingnews.com

Cypriot startup aims to reshape labour market with more transparent hiring

Cypriot startup Kyprium is focused on transforming the recruitment sector in Cyprus through artificial intelligence, promoting a more transparent and merit-based labour market. The platform offers AI-powered tools for both job seekers and employers to enhance recruitment efficiency.

Source: cyprus-mail.com

Avrea, co-founded by Aiven's Hannu Valtonen, raises €4 million for AI-native CI/CD platform

Avrea, a Helsinki-based startup focused on an AI-native CI/CD platform, has emerged from stealth and raised €4 million in pre-Seed funding.

Source: eu-startups.com

Railway raises $100 million to enhance AI-native cloud infrastructure

Railway, a cloud platform based in San Francisco, announced it raised $100 million in a Series B funding round to address the limitations of legacy cloud infrastructure amid rising demand for AI applications. The investment positions Railway as a notable infrastructure startup during the AI boom.

Source: VentureBeat AI

Salesforce launches new Slackbot AI agent

Salesforce has launched a rebuilt version of Slackbot, transforming it into an AI agent that can search enterprise data, draft documents, and take actions for employees. The new Slackbot is available to Business+ and Enterprise+ customers as part of Salesforce's strategy to enhance its products with artificial intelligence.

Source: VentureBeat AI
