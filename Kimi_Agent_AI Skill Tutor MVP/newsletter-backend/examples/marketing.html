Your morning briefing for Marketing · June 05, 2026

Good morning! Here's your AI briefing for Marketing.

The Roundup — The week's biggest moves, rounded up

The Big Story

Stensul launches MCP server

Stensul has announced the Stensul MCP Server Early Access Program, aimed at helping enterprise marketing teams implement governance in AI-assisted content creation, particularly for email. The program addresses the challenges of ensuring that AI-generated content meets brand, compliance, accessibility, and approval standards before reaching customers.

Source: stensul.com

Quick Hits

Optimizely and Deloitte Digital Collaborate on AI Marketing

Optimizely has announced a strategic technology collaboration with Deloitte Digital to enhance AI marketing and improve digital experiences for brands. The partnership aims to integrate AI orchestration with enterprise transformation expertise to support AI-led personalization and content strategies.

Source: aimagazine.com

Optimove Launches Optimove AI, the Only Marketing AI Suite That Brings AI Everywhere the Marketer Works

Optimove has launched Optimove AI, a marketing AI suite that operates within the platform, connects to external AI tools, and supports custom applications for marketers.

Source: thewisemarketer.com

Pollo AI Launches Marketing Studio with Five Workflows for AI Video Ads

Pollo AI has launched Marketing Studio, an AI-powered workspace that enables marketers to create video advertisements from various assets in a short time. The platform includes five workflows tailored to different marketing needs and offers 10 ad formats commonly used in performance marketing.

Source: prnewswire.com

Amadeus Launches Travel Advertising Platform

Amadeus has introduced the Amadeus Travel Advertising Platform, developed with Accenture, to assist travel brands in making advertising decisions based on emerging demand. The platform aims to enhance control, transparency, efficiency, and performance in travel advertising.

Source: amadeus.com

Zawa Launches AI Social Media Post Generator That Thinks Like a Brand Strategist

Zawa has launched an AI Social Media Post Generator designed for designers, marketers, and businesses, which creates a complete content set from a single product photo while maintaining brand visual identity. The tool serves both SMB owners as an AI marketing assistant and designers/marketers as a production tool for professional-grade social content.

Source: markets.businessinsider.com
