## Conflict Zones

### Market Size Variance
- AI Career Coach market: One source says $4.2B (2024) [Landscape], another $5.48B (2025) [Dim06]
  - Resolution: Temporal difference + definition variance. CAGR of 18.7-22.3% is consistent.

### Cohort Completion Rates
- Dim02 reports 85-96%, Dim08 reports 70-96%
  - Resolution: Selection bias in cohort programs (students self-select and pay premium). True rate likely 70-85% for quality programs. Dim02 may use marketing-inflated numbers.

### Newsletter Monetization
- Dim03 says paid tiers "largely failed" (Superhuman example)
- Dim12 says Lenny's Newsletter and Stratechery make paid tiers work
  - Resolution: Paid tiers work for individual thought leaders with strong personal brand, but fail for general news curation products. The user's product is closer to the former if it offers genuine utility.
