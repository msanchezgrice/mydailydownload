# My Daily Download — Design & Brand Brief

## Brand name

- **Spelling / capitalization:** **My Daily Download** (three words, title case). Lowercase domain form: `mydailydownload.com`. Facebook page handle: **Mydailydownload**.
- Do **not** render as "MyDailyDownload" (one word) in product copy except in the domain/handle. Do not use the prior working name "Daily AI Edge" anywhere user-facing.

## Taglines

- **Primary:** "AI news that actually matters to your career."
- **Supporting:** "Your daily AI briefing, personalized to your role."
- **Trust line (footer/hero subhead):** "Every item cites a real source."
- **Short social bio:** "The daily AI download for your job. Personalized by role + seniority. Real sources only."

## Voice / tone

- **Confident, useful, no hype.** A sharp colleague who already read everything and tells you the 3 things that matter to *your* job today.
- Plain, scannable, action-oriented ("do this today"). Avoids buzzwords, fake urgency, and fabricated stats.
- Trust-building and reliable — this is a daily email people let into their inbox. Professional, not flashy.
- One voice across all surfaces (landing, email, social, ads).

## Logo direction

- Wordmark-led. "My Daily Download" set in a clean geometric sans; optional mark = a downward download/arrow glyph or a downward chevron integrated into the "D", in amber (#F2A900) on the void background (#0B0C10).
- Must work as: full wordmark (email header, landing), compact mark (social avatar, favicon), monochrome (email-safe). No gradients in the email header (deliverability + dark-mode safety).

## Color palette

| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Void (primary bg / header)** | `#0B0C10` | App/landing dark background, email header band, CTA band |
| **Surface (elevated)** | `#14171D` | Cards, elevated panels, nav |
| **Amber (accent / brand)** | `#F2A900` | Logo, section labels, links, primary accent, stat values |
| **Mist (light text / UI)** | `#E6E8EE` | Primary text on dark surfaces |
| Muted slate | `#7A8194` | Secondary/meta text (existing email token) |
| Ink | `#1A1D23` | Body text on light email body |

Email body remains light (`#ffffff` content area) for inbox readability; dark void is the header/CTA frame + the web app shell. Carry the existing email tokens (`#7A8194`, `#1A1D23`, `#FFF8E7` tool-box tint) — they're already production-tuned.

## Typography

- **Direction:** Clean, highly readable sans-serif. Web/landing: Inter (or similar geometric grotesque) for UI + headings. Email: system stack already in template (`-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, sans-serif`) — keep for deliverability.
- Headings: tight, confident, 18–48px scale. Body: 14–16px, generous line-height (1.6–1.7) for scannability. Section labels: 11px uppercase, letter-spaced, amber.

## Key screens

1. **Landing (`/`)** — Void hero, amber accent, promise headline + email/career capture; "how it works" 3-step; 15-career grid; interactive sample-issue preview (real recent issue per career); pricing (Free + Pro $15–19); FAQ; trust band ("real sources only").
2. **Onboarding (`/onboarding`)** — Friendly multi-step (email + LinkedIn + resume → confirm parsed profile, correctable → delivery time + consent). Honest progress copy.
3. **Sample / archive issue** — Web-rendered email issue; "Your Daily Download for [Career]"; source links visible.
4. **Gated archive** — Rolling trailing 2-week list of the user's category issues.
5. **SEO category hub (`/ai-for/[career]`)** — Indexable, content-rich, "AI for [Profession]" hub refreshed weekly.
6. **Pricing / checkout / success / cancel** — Stripe-backed, one consistent price.

## Hero / first-viewport direction

- Void background (#0B0C10), amber wordmark top-left, single confident headline ("AI news that actually matters to your career"), one subhead with the trust line, one capture (email + career selector), and a peek at a real sample issue. No carousel, no fake social-proof counts — use real numbers only or a qualitative line.

## Product screenshots / image requirements

- Real rendered email issues per launch category (Marketing, Product, Founder) for landing + sample.
- Mobile + desktop email mockups (single-column, 640px).
- Archive and onboarding screen captures once live.

## OG image direction

- 1200×630, void background, amber wordmark, tagline "AI news that actually matters to your career", small career-grid motif. Per-category OG variants for `/ai-for/[career]` hubs ("AI for [Profession]").

## Social avatar / cover direction

- **Avatar:** compact download-glyph mark, amber on void, legible at 48px (brand master + 5 vertical accounts share the mark; vertical accounts add a category tag).
- **Cover:** void background, wordmark + tagline; per-vertical covers for the 5 X accounts (Marketing, Product, Founder, Sales, Design) with the category name in amber.
- **Facebook page (Mydailydownload):** matching avatar + cover.

## Ad creative direction (if/when ads in scope)

- Static + short-form: void background, one tailored headline per vertical ("The AI download for Marketers" / "…for PMs" / "…for Founders"), amber CTA. Lead with the personalization + real-sources angle, never fabricated stats. Paid ads spend is **gated** (see approval-boundaries) — creative direction only at this stage.
