01Strategy Snapshot

Why the ASL Tutor messaging transfers: both products sell personalized mastery of a fast-moving skill domain. Here the domain is AI's impact on your specific job.

Core promise

"AI is changing your job. Know first."

A daily 5-minute briefing personalized to your career (15 verticals) and seniority (3 tiers). Not generic tech news — your corner of the industry, every morning at 7 AM.

Messaging pillars (in order)

1. The Edge — people who see AI changes first pull ahead of peers in their field. (Primary — mirrors ASL winner)

2. Personalization — built for your role + seniority; drop your LinkedIn and it knows your job.

3. Trust — every item cites a real primary source. Zero hype, zero fabricated facts.

4. Effortless — 5-minute read, 7 AM, free to start.

Audience

Ambitious mid-career professionals (25–45) in the 15 verticals: Product, Marketing, Sales, Ops, HR, Design, Finance, Engineering, Data Science, Customer Success, Content, Consulting, Legal, Healthcare, Entrepreneurship. They feel AI FOMO but are tired of generic hype newsletters.

Conversion event

Free signup (double opt-in) is the ad objective — don't sell the $19/mo Pro in cold creative. CTA language: "Get Your Edge — Free." Upsell happens in onboarding.

02Brand Voice

If the brand were a person: the sharpest, best-read colleague on your team — reads everything overnight, tells you only what matters, always shows their sources, never hypes.

Specific

We are: concrete about roles, tools, outcomes ("a PM gets product news, a nurse gets clinical AI news").
We're not: vague ("stay informed about technology!").

Grounded

We are: source-cited, factual, calm. "Every item cites a real source" is a product guarantee, not a slogan.
We're not: breathless, doom-y, "AI will replace you" clickbait.

Competitive

We are: edge-framed — readers get ahead of peers. Confident, slightly provocative.
We're not: fear-mongering or putting other professionals down.

Direct

We are: short sentences, active voice, "you" language, no filler.
We're not: jargon-heavy, exclamation-point salesy, emoji-stuffed.

08How to Run the Test

Structure: 1 campaign → 3 ad sets by angle (Edge A1+V1+IMG1/3 · Anti-generic A2+V3+IMG2 · Personalization A5+V4+IMG4). Add V5 UGC into whichever angle wins.

Landing pages: message-match — generic angles → mydailydownload.com; career-specific A5/IMG4 → /ai-for/[career] hubs (already server-rendered with real cited briefings).

Conversion: optimize for free signup (double opt-in confirm). GA4 G-X27FVHNW9T is live; tag links with UTMs per variant (utm_campaign=hf-test-v1, utm_content=A1-V1…).

Creative rules: hook in 2s · captions always on · 9:16 for Reels/Stories, 1:1 for feed · keep "Every item cites a real source" in at least one frame of every asset — it's the trust differentiator.
